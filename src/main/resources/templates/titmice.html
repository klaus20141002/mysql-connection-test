Why do people overspend?

There are plenty of reasons why people seemingly throw their money away, but most commonly:

  • They have no clue where they are spending excessively
  • And how much more they can actually spend.

People spend instinctively and, as humans, are susceptible to Optimism Bias. Thus, we tend to overestimate our quota.

With Titmice, you can learn to take charge of your bad spending habits just through simple expense tracking.

Easy Budgeting

Set your budget's amount and duration. Titmice will advise you on how much to spend daily.

Smart Expense Input

With machine learning techniques, Titmice can teach itself, based on your previous expenses, to make smart suggestions.

Rollover Concept - Chest

Using a rollover concept, Titmice will track your daily leftover budget and add them to your chest.

The chest gives you a indicator on how much you can exceed your daily budget. The larger your chest amount, the more buffer you have.

Budget Insights

Titmice gives you a insight to your expenses that you would have missed out easily like identifying out-of-the-ordinary expenses and budget breakdown.
